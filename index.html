      Is Lightning Lane Multi Pass Worth It?

      High-level summary for each Disney park in peak July.

      Magic Kingdom

      LLMP shines here: Seven Dwarfs, Peter Pan, Space Mountain, Big Thunder, Haunted Mansion, Jungle Cruise, Buzz. Expect ~4 hours saved on a full day. TRON still requires separate LLI. Verdict: Strong YES in July.

      EPCOT

      LLMP helps most with Remy, Frozen Ever After, Soarin’, Test Track (if open), and Mission: SPACE. Guardians of the Galaxy: Cosmic Rewind is LLI-only, not LLMP. On a well-planned day, expect ~2–3 hours saved. Verdict: Worth it if you want to hit all headliners without backtracking.

      Hollywood Studios

      LLMP is extremely valuable here: Slinky Dog Dash, Tower of Terror, Rock ’n’ Roller Coaster, Smugglers Run, Toy Story Mania. Rise of the Resistance is LLI-only. In peak July, standby lines here are brutal—LLMP can easily save 3–4 hours. Verdict: Very strong YES.

      Animal Kingdom

      LLMP mainly helps with Kilimanjaro Safaris, Expedition Everest, and Na’vi River Journey. Avatar Flight of Passage is LLI-only. Because AK hours are shorter and crowds thin after midday, savings are more modest (~1.5–2 hours). Verdict: Nice-to-have; prioritize MK and HS first if budget is tight.
