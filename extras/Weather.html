Weather & Contingency

Rain • Heat • Lightning

General Strategy

  • Florida weather changes fast — always have ponchos.
  • Use indoor attractions during storms.
  • Take midday breaks during extreme heat.

Rain Plan

  • Magic Kingdom: Haunted Mansion, Pirates, PhilharMagic
  • EPCOT: The Seas, The Land, Imagination
  • Hollywood Studios: Frozen Sing‑Along, Runaway Railway
  • Animal Kingdom: Pandora attractions, indoor shows

Heat Plan

  • Indoor queues + shops
  • Hydrate constantly
  • Use cooling towels
