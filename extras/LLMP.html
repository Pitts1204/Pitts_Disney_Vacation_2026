LLMP Strategy

Lightning Lane • Multi‑Pass • Refreshing

Core Rules

  • Book your first LLMP at 7:00 AM sharp.
  • Refresh every 10–20 seconds at drop times.
  • Stack evening passes when possible.
  • Use downtime to refresh for better return times.

Best Uses

  • Magic Kingdom: Peter Pan, Jungle Cruise, Big Thunder
  • EPCOT: Remy, Test Track, Soarin’
  • Hollywood Studios: Slinky Dog, Runaway Railway
  • Animal Kingdom: Flight of Passage, Safaris
