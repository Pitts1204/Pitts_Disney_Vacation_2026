  • 3:20 AM: Wake up, showers, get dressed, final packing, confirm IDs, MagicBands, chargers, kids’ backpacks.

  • 4:05 AM: Load car (luggage, car seats, stroller, snacks, entertainment).
  • 4:10 AM: Depart home for SGF long-term parking.
  • 4:30 AM: Arrive SGF long-term parking, park, unload, walk/shuttle to terminal.
  • 4:35–5:00 AM: Check bags and clear security (bag cutoff is 45 minutes before departure).
