Detailed Itinerary – Wake to Bed

Morning – Home to SGF

  • 4:30 AM: Wake up, showers, get dressed, final packing, confirm IDs, MagicBands, chargers, kids’ backpacks.
  • 5:10 AM: Final house prep (trash, thermostat, doors/windows, quick tidy).
  • 5:20 AM: Load car (luggage, car seats, stroller, snacks, entertainment).
  • 5:25 AM: Depart home for SGF long-term parking.
  • 5:45 AM: Arrive SGF long-term parking, park, unload, walk/shuttle to terminal.
  • 5:50–6:00 AM: Check bags and clear security (bag cutoff is 45 minutes before departure).

Flights – SGF → ATL → MCO

  • 6:00–8:54 AM: Delta 1680 – SGF to ATL (1h 54m).
  • 8:54–10:25 AM: Layover in ATL (1h 31m) – restroom, snacks, short walk.
  • 10:25 AM–12:00 PM: Delta 664 – ATL to MCO (1h 35m).

Midday – MCO to Jetty Park Beach

  • 12:00–12:30 PM: Deplane, restrooms, walk to rental car center.
  • 12:30–1:00 PM: Pick up rental car, install car seats, set GPS/CarPlay.
  • 1:00 PM: Depart MCO for Jetty Park Beach.
  • 2:00–3:30 PM: Jetty Park Beach – beach time, pier walk, snacks, photos.

Afternoon – Jetty Park to Polynesian

  • 3:30 PM: Depart Jetty Park for Disney's Polynesian Village Resort.
  • 4:45–5:00 PM: Arrive at Polynesian, park, enter Great Ceremonial House.
  • 5:00–5:30 PM: Check-in, receive room, drop bags, quick room tour.
  • 5:30–6:00 PM: Freshen up, change for dinner, optional quick stop at Club Lounge for a drink or snack.

Evening – Narcoossee's Dinner

  • 6:00 PM: Leave room, walk to Great Ceremonial House, board Resort Monorail.
  • 6:20–6:30 PM: Arrive at Grand Floridian, walk to Narcoossee's.
  • 6:40–8:10 PM: Dinner at Narcoossee's.
