Jetty Park Beach — Cape Canaveral

~50 min from MCO · ~60 min from Polynesian

Why Jetty Park is Perfect for the Boys

  • Protected cove with calm, shallow water
  • No rip currents · gradual sandy slope
  • Tidal pools with crabs, barnacles, mussels, tiny fish
  • Lifeguards on duty in summer
  • Restrooms, showers, playground, picnic tables
  • Parking ~$15/day

Tips for Jetty Park

  • Arrive early afternoon for best parking
  • Bring water shoes — tidal pool rocks can be sharp
  • Check tide chart: low tide = best tidal pool exploring
  • SpaceX launches often visible from the beach

Beach Essentials

Sunscreen SPF 50+, towels, water bottles, snacks, swim gear, buckets, shovels, water shoes, change of clothes.
