Magic Kingdom – Day 2

Deep‑dive plan, routing, and tips

Morning Plan

Front‑load headliners while energy and wait times are best.

  • Rope drop: Seven Dwarfs Mine Train
  • Then: Peter Pan’s Flight
  • Then: Winnie the Pooh / Fantasyland loop

Afternoon Options

Heat + crowds = shows, indoor rides, and flexible routing.

  • Option A: Indoor shows + Carousel of Progress
  • Option B: Character meets + low‑intensity rides
  • Option C: Resort break, then return for evening

Evening Plan

  • Early dinner near hub
  • Fireworks viewing spot 30–45 minutes early
  • Post‑fireworks: low‑wait rides or exit depending on kids’ energy

Tips for MK Day 2

  • Use early hours for Fantasyland and headliners.
  • Keep an eye on parade times to avoid Main Street congestion.
  • Have a rain backup: indoor attractions clustered by land.
