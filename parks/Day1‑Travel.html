🌦 Weather Contingency Plan

Rain Plan: If arrival storms delay check‑in, head to Disney Springs for indoor shopping and dining.

Heat Plan: Stay hydrated and use Polynesian’s lobby cool‑down area before park entry.

Lightning Plan: Avoid outdoor transport loops during storms; use monorail instead.

Break Plan: If the boys need a rest, watch the Magic Kingdom fireworks from the beach instead of inside the park.

⚡ LLMP Prep Tips for Arrival Day

  • Book Lightning Lanes for Magic Kingdom starting at 7 AM ET while in transit.
  • Prioritize Seven Dwarfs Mine Train and Peter Pan’s Flight for Day 2.
  • Use the Delta Wi‑Fi connection to refresh LLMP availability during layover in ATL.
  • Check Genie+ tips in the app before landing in Orlando.
